burkmak

Read-it-later, kept warm

A quiet home for everything you mean to read.

Save anything from the web, read it clean, and keep your library truly yours.

  • Save anything

    Paste a link or share from any app. burkmak grabs the full article and keeps it — even if the original goes dark.

  • Clean reader view

    No ads, no clutter. Just Literata, generous margins, and the highlights you leave behind.

  • Sync to Kobo & export to Obsidian

    Send a piece to your e-reader for the evening, then push your notes straight into your vault.
